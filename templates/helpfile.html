An ASCII file with a list of names and/or position pair coordinates. The names will be resolved by one the resolver (NED/Simbad). To force the search with an IRAM name, it should be enclosed by two * (e.g. *M83A*). The positions are in the format Ra Dec(e.g. 12:12:12.12 30:30:30.30). Note that right ascension coordinates must be given in hours but declination in sexagesimal degrees.
